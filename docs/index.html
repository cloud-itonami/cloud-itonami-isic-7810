Community Employment Agency governed

The problem: Employment agencies face regulatory liability when matching and placing candidates — discriminatory matches, fee miscalculations, and unverified work authorization can expose agencies to civil-rights violations and compliance penalties.

Our differentiator: An independent Employment Agency Governor verifies every match and placement against legal requirements. Discriminatory matching bases, placement-fee mismatches, and unverified work authorizations trigger HARD holds that no human approval can override—preventing unsafe commits before they happen.

What it is not: This does not hold a jurisdiction's operating license (your responsibility), nor does it replace candidate sourcing, screening, or fit judgment. It is the compliance scaffold—independent verification that catches regulatory violations before deployment.

What this guarantees

  • No candidacy with discriminatory matching criteria will be placed
  • Placement fees always match independent recomputation (salary × fee rate)
  • Candidates requiring work authorization cannot be placed without verification
  • Matching and placement are never autonomous—always require human approval
  • Every decision is permanently logged to the audit ledger
